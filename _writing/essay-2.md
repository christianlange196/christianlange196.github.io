## Thesis
Models are indispensable, but confidence should scale with evidence quality and domain volatility.

## Argument and Analysis
Model outputs can appear precise while hiding fragile assumptions. Decision quality improves when teams make assumptions explicit and design for updating beliefs as new evidence arrives.

Scientific humility is operational: track uncertainty, test alternatives, and reward correction rather than certainty theater.
