## Current Role
Postdoctoral Researcher at Purdue University (Quantum Photonic Integrated Design Center, QuPIDC), working with Dr. Libai Huang.

## Education
- PhD, Physics (2025): Purdue University. Thesis: *Light-Matter Interactions in Organic Molecules*.
- BS, Physics (2017): Utah State University. Minors in Mathematics and Computer Science. GPA: 3.99/4.00.

## Focus Areas
- Quantum optics and nanophotonics
- Optical instrumentation and confocal microscopy
- Laser/cavity systems and precision alignment
- Experiment automation with Python and custom hardware control
- Materials synthesis and emitter integration
